What is the Verto Platform

The Verto platform was created to service a growing need from remitters to avoid excessive fees and time associated with sending FIAT currencies across boarders. The way Verto platform works is such that a user logs into a partner remitters platform, they perform a transfer in the normal process. The remitter will transfer funds to Verto accounts and the transaction gets recorded on the blockchain and smart contracts initiate the transaction. On the receiving end the Verto account transfers the exchanged funds back to remitter accounts for processing and transfer to the receiving user. All this occurs within seconds!

User signs up on the remitters applications and initiates a funds transfer.
Remitter transfers funds to the appropriate Verto Account.
Transaction recorded on the blockchain.
Funds released to Remitter at destination
Remitter releases funds to user.

Fast, Simple, Secure

Why Hold Verto Tokens

Verto is an ERC20 token and as part of the Token creation, holders of the token will be able to use the Verto Token to pay for remittance fees with certain providers and for those that assist in the mining process will be rewarded with tokens that help mine the transaction into the blockchain. Remitters submit a transaction and appropriate token fee to process the transaction on the platform. This token fee is in return paid to miners to assist in the transaction validation and processing.
