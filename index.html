THE INTERNET OF INDEPENDENT WORK IS HERE

TiiQu is a blockchain based platform that enables creation of a digital “passport” serving as proof of an individual’s identity, qualifications, certifications, memberships, previous work experience, performance metrics and education. The TQ (Trust Quotient) is an algorithmically calculated score measuring professional trustworthiness derived from claims certified by third parties. It can be used as the avatar for an individual’s achievements. The TiiQu platform enables global collaboration without middlemen.

It removes guesswork and assumptions about an individual’s claims. Members can establish collaboration agreements via easy to author smart contracts, executing payment and enforcing conditions in real time and at a fraction of current costs. Best contributors of the community who achieve a high trust quotient ranking are empowered to purchase a stake in the platform.

Features

Verification

Professional Passport

Proof of an individual’s identity, qualifications, certifications, memberships and previous work experience.

Claims Verification

Removes the guesswork and assumptions about an individual’s professional claims.

Trust Quotient

Measures an individual’s professional trustworthiness via a continuously updated score based on verified evidence.

Collaboration

Smart Contracts

Non-technical users can author blockchain based smart contracts that enforce conditions parties agree to.

Tokenized Transactions

Users may choose any currency for transactions. The underlying system will make conversions and transfer the appropriate value to the receiver. International transactions in any currency will occur in real-time at a fraction of the cost of current practices.

Dispute Resolution

The platform will allow contracting parties to select a crowd-sourced arbiter to mediate disputes.
