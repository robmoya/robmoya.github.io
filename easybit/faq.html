What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is a type of digital currency in which encryption techniques are used to regulate the units of currency and verify the transfer of funds. Bitcoin operates independently of a central bank, instead it uses a decentrailzed system to verify transactions. Some people use Bitcoin as a alternative to fiat currencys or to buy other cryptocurrency’s.

What are Bitcoin/Crypto wallets?

All cryptocurrency’s are stored in crypto wallets. Crypto wallet’s are software programs where Cryptocurrency’s are stored. To be technically accurate, Cryptocurrencys are not stored anywhere; there is a private key (secret number) for every crypto address that is saved in the Crypto wallet of the person who owns the balance.
